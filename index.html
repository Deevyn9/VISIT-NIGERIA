A little dive into Nigeria

Overview

Nigeria is a West-African country situated on the gulf of guinea, it is a country that can boast of many landmarks and wild-life reserves. Nigeria is the most populous country in africa and it also has the largest economy.

Our Culture

Nigeria is a country inhabited by over 250 ethnic groups, the largest ethnic groups are the Igbo, Hausa and Yoruba. These ethnic groups speak over 500 languages. They also have a variety of cuisines, fashion styles and festivals.
